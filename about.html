ABOUT ME

I’m a User Experience Product Designer. Currently, my role as a Senior UX/UI Product Designer, I am daily involved in creating web design experiences that help people find what they are looking for in a delightful way. I really find my passion in problem solving, learning from others outside my team to establish creative strategies, while staying aligned with new tools to design efficiently. In summary, I follow through on a design vision by adapting to challenging requirements while maintaining the integrity of the company's design system. I stay motivated in my work with a good laugh and a taro boba smoothie break!


SKILL SET

Over 12 years of experience working at Ticketmaster starting as a Production Artist and currently a Senior UX/ UI Designer. Strong history in User Experience, Visual Design, Responsive Design, Visioning, eCommerce, A/B Testing, Usability Testing, and Prototyping. 

My toolbox: SketchApp, Invisionapp, Zeplin, Jira, Trello, Adobe Creative Suite, OptimalWorkshop, UsabilityHub.com, UserTesting.com
